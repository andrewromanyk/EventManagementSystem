What I, as a customer, can do?

What I, as an event manager, can do?
You can create, edit, and delete events. Additionally, you can manage tickets.
Is there a limit for tickets or events?
No, you may buy as many tickets or create as many events as you want or need. Though, be mindful, that abuse of this feature can lead to a ban.
